Humanity - Arunachal is a public charity registered u/s 12AA of the Income-tax Act, 1961 with the Commissioner of Income-tax (Exemption), Kolkata. Since its registration, it has been taking activities of various kinds to benefit the general public and the poor and needy.


  • Promotion & diffusion of useful knowledge
  • Promote & carry out programs for the benefit of children, women & under privileged
  • Promotion of women empowerment
  • Work for the rural development
  • Awareness generation on social evils
  • Advancement of any other object of general public utility
